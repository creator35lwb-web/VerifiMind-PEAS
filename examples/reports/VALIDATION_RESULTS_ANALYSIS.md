## Recommendations for AI Builders

### If You're Building AI Applications:

**1. Start with Education or Developer Tools**
- Highest approval rates (100% and 80%)
- Clear value propositions
- Lower ethical/legal risks

**2. Prioritize Privacy-First Design**
- Mental Health app scored 8.0 with privacy focus
- On-device processing when possible
- Explicit consent mechanisms

**3. Augment, Don't Replace**
- All approved concepts enhance human capabilities
- Avoid decision-making in high-stakes domains
- Keep humans in the loop

**4. Address Ethics Early**
- 30% of concepts vetoed for ethical reasons
- Build safeguards from day one
- Consider Z Agent perspective in design

**5. Focus on Clear ROI**
- Top concepts show measurable value (time saved, quality improved)
- Quantify benefits: "Reduce X by Y%"
