## The Concept Scrutinizer: Core Methodology

### Four-Step Validation Process

#### Step 1: Clarification & Definition
- Restate user's core concept
- Identify key assumptions
- Define target users and scenarios
- Establish success criteria

#### Step 2: Multi-Dimensional Feasibility Analysis
- **Innovation**: Is it disruptive, incremental, or recombinative?
- **Technical Feasibility**: What technologies are needed? What are the bottlenecks?
- **Market Potential**: How large is the target market? Is this a real pain point?
- **Risk Assessment**: Execution, competition, ethical, legal risks

#### Step 3: Socratic Challenge & Validation
- Challenge every assumption
- Find counterexamples and failure cases
- Identify cognitive biases
- Test extreme scenarios

#### Step 4: Strategic Recommendations & Roadmap
- Synthesize analysis into actionable conclusions
- Provide 3-5 executable strategy options
- Create detailed implementation timeline
- Establish feedback loops
