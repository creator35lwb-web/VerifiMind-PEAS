### **Daily Content Types**

**Monday**: Methodology discussions, best practices

**Wednesday**: Research and theory, academic discussions  
**Thursday**: Community spotlights, case studies  
**Friday**: Weekly recap, preview next week

**40%**: Educational (guides, tutorials, best practices)

**20%**: Product (roadmap, features, updates)  
**10%**: Fun (memes, polls, off-topic)

## Success Metrics

### **Quantitative Metrics**

**GitHub**:
- Stars: Target 100+ (Week 1: 20, Week 2: 40, Week 3: 60, Week 4: 100)
- Discussions: Target 20+ (Week 1: 5, Week 2: 10, Week 3: 15, Week 4: 20)
- Comments: Target 100+ (Week 1: 20, Week 2: 40, Week 3: 60, Week 4: 100)
- Forks: Target 10+ (Week 1: 2, Week 2: 4, Week 3: 7, Week 4: 10)

**Social Media**:
- Twitter/X impressions: Target 500+ per week
- LinkedIn views: Target 200+ per week
- Profile visits: Target 50+ per week

**Community**:
- Active participants: Target 50+
- Case studies shared: Target 5+
- Active contributors: Target 10+

### **Qualitative Metrics**

**Engagement quality**:
- Are discussions substantive?
- Are questions being answered?
- Are users sharing case studies?
- Is community helping each other?

**Community health**:
- Are new members welcomed?
- Are contributors recognized?
- Are conflicts resolved constructively?
- Is moderation fair and consistent?

### **Monthly Review Questions**

1. Did we hit quantitative targets?
2. Is engagement quality high?
3. Is community health good?
4. What worked well?
5. What needs improvement?
6. What should we do next month?
