A comprehensive fitness tracking application for runners and cyclists.

Users should be able to:
- Log daily workouts with distance, time, and route information
- Set weekly and monthly fitness goals
- Track their progress over time with charts and statistics
- Share achievements with friends on social media
- Compete in challenges with other users
- Get AI-powered training recommendations based on their performance

The app should have:
- Mobile-friendly responsive design
- Dark mode support
- Integration with GPS for route tracking
- Cloud sync across devices
- Privacy controls for sharing data
