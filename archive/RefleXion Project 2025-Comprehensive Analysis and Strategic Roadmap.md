## 2. Triple AI Collaboration Model Implementation

### Architecture Design

The VerifiMind Triple AI Model advances beyond YSense's static assignments to create dynamic, self-improving collaboration:

**X Intelligent (X主导 - Innovation Lead)**
- **Primary Role**: Creative strategy development and innovation leadership
- **Capabilities**: Advanced reasoning, strategic planning, creative problem-solving, self-reflection on outcomes
- **Authority**: Leads during innovation phase but must pass verification

**Z Guardian (Verification Component)**  
- **Primary Role**: Multi-perspective validation and quality assurance
- **Capabilities**: Risk assessment, compliance verification, error detection, cross-referencing with standards
- **Authority**: Can request modifications but cannot override safety decisions

**CS Security (Safety & Ethics Authority)**
- **Primary Role**: Security, safety, and ethical oversight
- **Capabilities**: Threat assessment, bias detection, safety protocol enforcement, values alignment
- **Authority**: Veto power on safety/ethical violations

### Dynamic Interaction Model
Unlike YSense's sequential processing, VerifiMind implements **parallel collaborative verification** with structured conflict resolution protocols ensuring all three components work simultaneously rather than in sequence.
